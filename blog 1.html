Now we are going to learn about Aglio e Olio Spaghetti. So lets start.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Red Pepper flakes
  • Optional - Cheese and Basil leaves

Recipe

  1. Boil the spaghetti untli its al dente
  2. Heat the olive oil in a pan and Suate the garlic in it until its golden brown
  3. Next we are going to put chilli flakes in it.
  4. Some pasta water and then the pasta itself.
  5. Lastly will add some salt and cheese and garnish it with some basil leaves, totally optional.
